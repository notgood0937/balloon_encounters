The signal engine (`src/lib/signalEngine.ts`) generates signals by combining price movements, wallet activity, news data, and volume patterns within Balloon Encounters.

## Signal Reference

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="👑 top_wallet_entry">
    A **top-50 PnL wallet** opens a new position. Tracks the highest-performing traders on Polymarket.

    - **Source:** `signalEngine.ts`
    - **Trigger:** New position detected from a top-50 ranked wallet
    - **Strength:** Based on wallet PnL rank and position size
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🎯 top_cluster">
    **3+ top-50 wallets** buy the same market within 1 hour. Indicates coordinated smart money conviction.

    - **Source:** `signalEngine.ts`
    - **Trigger:** Cluster of 3+ top wallets in same market within 1hr window
    - **Strength:** Scales with number of wallets and total volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📰 news_catalyst">
    News article matched to a market that also has **recent smart money activity**. Combines information edge with money flow.

    - **Source:** `signalEngine.ts`
    - **Trigger:** AI-matched news + concurrent wallet activity
    - **Strength:** Based on news relevance score and trade volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🐋 whale_accumulation">
    Sustained buying from **whale wallets** over multiple trades. Signals conviction rather than a single large bet.

    - **Source:** `smartSignals.ts`
    - **Trigger:** Multiple buys from whale addresses over time
    - **Strength:** Weighted by total accumulated value
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="⚡ smart_divergence">
    Smart wallet positions **diverge significantly** from the current market price. The smart money disagrees with the crowd.

    - **Source:** `smartSignals.ts`
    - **Trigger:** >15% divergence between smart money avg price and market price
    - **Strength:** Proportional to divergence magnitude
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="👥 cluster_activity">
    Multiple wallets in a **known cluster** trade the same market in a short window. May indicate coordinated group activity.

    - **Source:** `smartSignals.ts`
    - **Trigger:** 2+ cluster wallets trading same market within timeframe
    - **Strength:** Based on cluster size and trade velocity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🔄 momentum_shift">
    Sudden change in **trade volume, direction, or velocity**. Detects when a market is breaking out of its range.

    - **Source:** `smartSignals.ts`
    - **Trigger:** Volume or price velocity exceeds rolling average by 2σ
    - **Strength:** Based on deviation from baseline
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Signal Sources

<Tabs items={["Base Signals", "Unified Engine"]}>
  <Tab value="Base Signals">
    `src/lib/smartSignals.ts` provides **4 base signal types**:
    - `whale_accumulation`
    - `smart_divergence`
    - `cluster_activity`
    - `momentum_shift`

    These are computed from wallet activity data and price snapshots stored in SQLite.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Unified Engine">
    `src/lib/signalEngine.ts` wraps the base signals and adds **3 higher-level types**:
    - `top_wallet_entry` — requires leaderboard data
    - `top_cluster` — requires wallet clustering
    - `news_catalyst` — requires news + smart money correlation

    The unified engine is what `SignalPanel.tsx` calls via `generateSignals()`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Alert Integration

<Callout type="info">
Signals feed into the alert system at `src/hooks/useAlerts.ts`. Alerts have a **5-minute debounce** and persist in `localStorage`.
</Callout>

| Alert Type | Trigger |
|-----------|---------|
| `price_cross` | Price crosses a user-defined threshold |
| `new_market` | New market detected in sync |
| `smart_signal` | Any signal fires above minimum strength |
